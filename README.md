# Herfy Sales Performance Dashboard

This repository contains a comprehensive **Sales Performance Analysis for Herfy**, focusing on evaluating **net sales, returns, route efficiency, and monthly trends** using Power BI.

The dashboard and report are designed to support **data-driven decision-making** by highlighting strong-performing areas and identifying operational improvement opportunities.

---

## Project Overview

The objective of this project is to analyze Herfy’s sales data and provide insights into:

- Overall sales performance  
- Return rates and their impact on profitability  
- City-wise and route-wise sales distribution  
- Monthly sales and return trends  
- Route efficiency and risk identification  

An interactive Power BI dashboard is used to visualize KPIs and trends clearly for business users.

## Business Insights

### City Performance
- **Top Performing Cities:** Riyadh Cash, Jail, Riyadh KA  
- **Lowest Performing City:** Hail  
- Sales are heavily concentrated in Riyadh, indicating strong regional demand.

### Route Performance
- **Best Performing Routes:** Export, CCRS01, CCRS02  
- **Routes Requiring Attention:** CCR163, CCR157, CCRV88  
- High-return routes may indicate delivery, handling, or quality issues.

### Route Performance Color Coding
- **Green:** High sales with very low return percentage  
- **Yellow:** Moderate performance, requires monitoring  
- **Red:** High return percentage, needs immediate action  

---

## Monthly Trends Analysis

### Net Sales Trend
- Sales fluctuations observed throughout the year  
- Noticeable dips in **March** and **August**  
- Strong recovery in **May** and **September**  
- Forecast suggests **stable to moderate growth** ahead

### Return Percentage Trend
- Significant reduction from **19% in January** to **5% in May**  
- Indicates operational improvements and better route management  
- Slight increase later in the year but remains under control

---

## Conclusion

Herfy demonstrates **strong overall sales performance**, driven by high-demand cities and efficient routes.  
While most routes perform consistently well, a few high-return routes require focused operational improvements.

The declining return trend reflects **positive progress in quality control and logistics efficiency**, supporting confident planning for future periods.
